Acupuncture and Traditional Chinese Medicine (T.C.M.)

Acupuncture and Traditional Chinese Medicine (T.C.M.)

At Absolute Healing, Carol offers Acupuncture and Traditional Chinese Medicine as a time-tested approach that supports the body’s natural ability to restore balance, vitality, and overall wellbeing.

Traditional Chinese Medicine views health as a dynamic balance between the body, mind, emotions, and energy systems.

Acupuncture works by stimulating specific points along the body’s meridians to improve the flow of Qi (life force energy), reduce blockages, and promote harmony within the body’s systems.

Treatments are guided by an individualised assessment that considers physical symptoms, emotional wellbeing, lifestyle factors, and energetic patterns.

Acupuncture may be integrated with complementary techniques to support the nervous system, hormonal balance, digestion, immune function, and overall resilience.

Acupuncture Facial Rejuvenation

Acupuncture Facial Rejuvenation

Acupuncture Facial Rejuvenation is a natural, holistic approach to facial rejuvenation that supports both beauty and overall wellbeing.

Rooted in Traditional Chinese Medicine, this treatment enhances circulation, supports lymphatic drainage, and stimulates collagen by placing fine, sterile needles at specific points on the face and body.

It can help improve tone and texture, soften fine lines, reduce puffiness, and promote a healthy, radiant complexion.

For best results, a minimum of 3 sessions is recommended.

Fire Cupping

Fire Cupping

Fire Cupping is a traditional technique used to support circulation, release muscle tension, and promote the body’s natural healing response.

During a session, glass cups are briefly warmed and placed on the skin to create gentle suction.

This helps lift and decompress tissues, improve blood and lymphatic flow, and release stagnation that may be contributing to pain, tightness, or restricted movement.

Fire cupping is commonly used as part of pain management and can support relief from muscle and joint discomfort, stiffness, headaches, stress, and fatigue.

Temporary circular marks may appear and typically fade within a few days.
